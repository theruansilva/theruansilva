Coffee-fueled startup builder ☕😎 Nice to meet you! 👋

I got into technology when I was 12 years old. I found a tool that let me write JavaScript on my jurassic laptop 🦕 and instantly see the results on my phone. 📱👈

When I turned 18, I co-founded an esports organization with a few friends. We landed sponsors, signed players, and even made money. 💵😎 With almost no business experience, we grew way too fast—and eventually had to shut it down. Looking back, that experience became the foundation for everything that came next. ☁️🌈

After that, I decided to pursue a career in software. My goal was to build companies using technology as my main tool. 😄 I started with the fundamentals—React, Node.js, MongoDB, PostgreSQL, TypeScript, and everything else I needed to break into the industry. 🏹🎯

My first opportunity was at a startup called Clube do Tennis, where I accepted a salary of R$0 in exchange for experience. 🙃 It turned out to be one of the best decisions I could have made. Under the CTO's mentorship, I grew tremendously—not only because I put theory into practice, but because I was constantly asking questions. Kafka, Node.js, Remix.js, React Native, and event-driven architecture were some of the biggest game changers I learned there. 🤯

Since then, I've worked at software houses, built products of my own, and I still believe that technology can create simple solutions for a complex world. 🧑💻

There are plenty more stories to tell, but if you've made it this far, I'd love to connect and share them with you. 😄
